Our food

At Salt and Stone, we believe that great food begins with great ingredients. Our menu is crafted with the finest locally-sourced produce, fresh-caught seafood, and ethically-raised meats. Every dish is a tribute to the rich flavors of nature, enhanced by the subtle yet bold seasoning of hand-harvested salts and fragrant herbs.

Our space is designed to evoke warmth and connection, whether you're enjoying a casual dinner with friends, celebrating a milestone, or simply savoring a quiet evening with a glass of wine. The Salt and Stones experience is as much about the ambiance as it is about the flavors on your plate. We are committed to sustainability and reducing our environmental impact. From our sourcing practices to the materials we use in our kitchen, we take pride in being a part of the solution for a healthier planet. Thank you for choosing Salt and Stone. We look forward to welcoming you and sharing our passion for exceptional dining.
