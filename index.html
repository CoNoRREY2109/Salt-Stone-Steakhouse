Welcome to Salt & Stone

At Salt & Stone, we’re passionate about delivering the ultimate steakhouse experience. Nestled in the heart of Glasnevin Dublin 9, we combine timeless culinary traditions with modern sophistication. Our menu features premium, hand-selected cuts of beef, expertly aged and grilled to perfection, alongside a curated selection of fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Whether you’re here for a celebratory dinner, a business gathering, or a casual night out, our warm atmosphere, exceptional service, and exquisite flavors create a dining experience to savor. At Salt & stone, every meal is more than just a dish – it’s a celebration of taste, quality, and hospitality.
